Privacy Policy

隱私權政策

生效日期 Effective date:2026-06-11

一句話版本:HoursTag 不收集、不傳輸、不分享你的任何資料。你輸入的一切,都只存在你自己的裝置上。
In one sentence: HoursTag does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything you enter stays on your own device.

01我們收集哪些資料?

不收集任何資料。HoursTag 沒有伺服器、不需要註冊帳號、不要求任何個人資訊。你在 App 中輸入的薪資、工時與目標,只儲存在你裝置的本機儲存空間,開發者完全無法存取。

What data do we collect? — None. HoursTag has no servers, requires no account, and asks for no personal information. Your salary, working hours, and goals are stored only in your device's local storage; the developer has no access to them.

02第三方追蹤與廣告

HoursTag 不包含任何廣告、不使用任何第三方分析或追蹤工具(無 Google Analytics、無 Facebook SDK、無任何廣告 SDK),也不會與任何第三方分享資料。

Third-party tracking & ads — HoursTag contains no ads, uses no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and shares no data with any third party.

03資料儲存與刪除

  • 所有資料只存在你的裝置本機。
  • 刪除 App 時,所有資料隨之永久刪除。
  • 若你啟用 iCloud 整機備份,App 資料會包含在你個人的裝置備份中;該備份由 Apple 的 iCloud 條款保護,開發者無法存取。
Storage & deletion — All data lives on your device only and is permanently deleted when you delete the app. With iCloud device backup enabled, app data is included in your personal backup, governed by Apple's iCloud terms; the developer cannot access it.

04購買資訊

HoursTag 為一次性買斷的付費 App。購買與付款完全由 Apple App Store 處理,開發者不會取得你的姓名、信用卡或帳單資訊。退款請透過 reportaproblem.apple.com 向 Apple 申請。

Purchases — HoursTag is a one-time paid app. All payments are processed by the Apple App Store; the developer never receives your name, card, or billing details. For refunds, contact Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

05網路連線

HoursTag 的核心功能完全離線運作,不需要網路連線即可使用。

Network — HoursTag's core features work fully offline; no internet connection is required.

06兒童隱私

HoursTag 是一款生產力工具,並非以兒童為目標對象。由於我們不收集任何資料,因此也不會收集任何兒童的個人資訊。

Children's privacy — HoursTag is a productivity tool not directed at children. Since we collect no data at all, we collect no data from children.

07政策變更

若未來政策有任何變更(例如新增需要說明的功能),我們會更新本頁面並調整上方的生效日期。重大變更會在 App 更新說明中載明。

Changes — Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date; material changes will be noted in App Store release notes.
